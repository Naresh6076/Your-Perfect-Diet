Quick Diet Tips

  • Start your day with a protein-rich breakfast.
  • Snack on nuts and fruits, not chips or sweets.
  • Stay hydrated – drink at least 2 liters of water daily.
  • Cut down on sugary drinks and processed food.
  • Include more green vegetables in every meal.
  • Track your portions – don't overeat even healthy food.
  • Move your body – even a walk counts!
